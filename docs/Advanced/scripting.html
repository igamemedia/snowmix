Advanced Scripting

Updated for Snowmix 0.5.0

Introduction


Snowmix offers both basic scripting and advanced scripting. This page will over time present how to do advanced scripting and it is recommended that the concepts for basic scripting is well understood before advanced scripting is undertaken.


Advanced scripting in Snowmix means taking advantage of the built-in Tcl Interpreter. and using it for executing complex command sets possibly depending of a complex set of states. Determining the state, scripts executed by the built-in interpreter may involve queries into internal Snowmix settings.
